# Canonical: PV cost evidence handling

## Decision or rule
- Keep one canonical summary for PV cost assumptions.
- Link every claim to a source note or source file.
- If two sources disagree, keep both references and mark confidence lower.

## When to use
- While answering cost comparison questions in demo.
- During updates of energy-related assumptions.

## Known trade-offs
- Canonical note improves answer quality but needs periodic review.
- Conservative confidence prevents overclaiming, but may look less decisive.
